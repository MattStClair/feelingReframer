Project background

With my background as a mental health therapist, it was my goal to build an application that I thought of prior to beginning as a developer.

The feeling reframer's idea is based on concepts borrowed from a psychological therapy approach known as Narrative Therapy. Narrative therapy is based on the post modern principle that our reality is created by the words we use to describe it.

As a therapist, I find work with people who come in with a limited emotional vocabulary, which making it difficlu to express theirselves accurately. Thus, it is important to be able to have synonyms to express accurately what is truly being felt.
